
March 2023 - Version 4: 

I. A new table with all station locations was added, filename SFBayStations.csv
II. An edited station map was added, with a few more stations labeled, filename SFBay_station_map_v4.pdf
III. Revisions found in the Version 4 data file SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData1969-2015v4.csv:
1. Added the time stamp and Julian date for each date, station and depth of sampling
2. Three new parameters of data were added, each of these were derived from measured parameters: ratio of chlorophyll-a and pheopigments, oxygen percent saturation, and density as Sigma-t 
3. Silicate results for 3/11/2014- 12/16/2015 were edited with a salinity correction which changed the concentrations 0-10% from the original (uncorrected) results. The following equation was applied: corrected silicate = uncorrected silicate/((dilution adjusted salinity * -0.4595 + 103.79)/100). "Dilution adjusted salinity" is based on how much the sample was diluted for nutrient analysis.
4. Added newly discovered archived full cruises and one additional station on one date: 12/8/1998, 4/14/1999, and 8/9/2005 all stations, and station 30 on 2/2/2005. 
5. Added nutrient data: 1388 lines of data (single station and single depth on a single date) were added, additions include all nutrient parameters or a subset depending on the date/station/depth. These nutrient data were previously omitted due to a variety of causes from database errors to recently discovered archived data. 
6. Two dates were changed/corrected by one day and nutrient data were added for both of these cruise dates (these nutrient data were not included in the #5 count): 9/30/91 was changed to 10/1/91; 11/20/91 was changed to 11/19/91. 
7. Discrete data were added for 12/9/1997 (whole cruise) and 2/23/2010 stations 3 and 2.  Discrete lab analyzed data (chlorophyll, oxygen, SPM, nutrients) and salinity and temperature collected near-surface with non CTD sensors were used as CTD data were not collected at these date/locations.
8. Duplicate lines were removed for 3/17/98 station 29 at 2m and 3/19/2002 station 2 at 2m


May 2017 - Version 3: 
We discovered that a small subset of the silicate measurements made between January 2011 and February 2014 have questionable reliability. These data were removed and not present in the revised file: SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData1969-2015v3.csv


December 2016 - Version 2:
We discovered inconsistencies in the format of sampling dates listed in the original data file: SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData1969-2015.csv.  These were corrected in the data file: SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData1969-2015v2.csv
