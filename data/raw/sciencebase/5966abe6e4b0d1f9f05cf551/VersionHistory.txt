
February 2023 - version 4.0

In this version the following edits and additions were made: 

v.4.0 (1) The citation was changed to:  Schraga, T.S., Nejad, E.S., Eddy, T., Martin, C.A., and Cloern, J.E., 2018, USGS measurements of water quality in San Francisco Bay (CA), 2016-2021 (ver. 4.0, February 2023): U.S. Geological Survey data release, https://doi.org/10.5066/F7D21WGF.

v.4.0 (2) The separate files for each year of data were compiled into a single data file with the filename 2016_2021SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData.csv. 2020 and 2021 data were added, and the 2016, 2017, 2018, and 2019 data were updated as detailed below.

v.4.0 (3) The following calculated parameters were added for all years: Ratio of chlorophyll-a and pheopigments, oxygen percent saturation, and density as Sigma-t 

v.4.0 (4) For 2019 data, the National Water Quality Lab reported the following data's reliability was questionable and we removed them from the dataset:
PHOSPHATE removed from 2M depth: 4/25/19 station 649, and 7/1/19 station 657 
NITRITE at 2m depth removed from the following stations and 2019 dates: 
stations 18,22,24,27,30,32 nitrite were removed from 4/18, 4/25, 5/14, 5/28, 6/4, 6/25, 7/1, 7/24
stations 3,6,9,13,15 nitrite were removed from 4/25, 5/14, 6/4, 7/1
station 36 nitrite was removed from 4/18, 4/25, 5/14, 5/28, 6/25, 7/24
station 34 nitrite was removed from 6/4 and 7/1
station 649 nitrite was removed from 5/14, 6/4, 7/1

Fv.4.0 (5) Nutrient data were added for the 12/19/19 cruise
 
v.4.0 (6) Nutrient data depths were changed from 1m to 2m for the following stations and dates: 4/1/16 station 24, 4/15/16 station 32, 8/24/16 stations 32 and 6

v.4.0 (7) Discrete chlorophyll data were added for 2/17/17 station 30 at 2m

v.4.0 (8) In previous versions the 2016 data had a column named Calculated_Oxygen, in this version that same column is simply named Oxygen. For 2016, the data in this column were calculated from regression, for 2017-2021 the data were direct from the sensor. 

v.4.0 (9) The station locations in the SFBay_TableofStationLocations.V4.0.csv was updated to have decimal latitude and longitude



March 26, 2020 - version 3.0

v.3.0 (1)
2016 and 2017 Silicate (Si) data were updated. An analytical error was discovered by the National Water Quality Lab which was corrected by applying a salinity correction. Si in prior versions have incorrect concentrations 0-10% lower than the corrected value. Previous versions of the Si data may be obtained by emailing authors Schraga or Nejad.

v.3.0 (2)
2016 data file with corrected Si was added, filename 2016v.3.0SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData.csv
2017 data file with corrected Si was added, filename 2017v.3.0SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData.csv
2018 data file was added, filename 2018SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData.csv
2019 data file was added, filename 2019SanFranciscoBayWaterQualityData.csv
Note for 2019 data: at the time of this publication nutrients results are not available for 12/19/19, those data will be included in the next version of this data product

v.3.0 (3) 
The citation was changed to: Schraga, T.S., Nejad, E.S., Martin, C.A., and Cloern, J.E., 2018, USGS measurements of water quality in San Francisco Bay (CA), beginning in 2016 (ver. 3.0, March 2020): U.S. Geological Survey data release, https://doi.org/10.5066/F7D21WGF.
